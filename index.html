About Kayla

Confidence that books the shot

Kayla Rockett is a swimwear and lingerie model based in Las Vegas. Born and raised in New Jersey, she brings East Coast edge and Las Vegas energy to every set.

Versatile in front of the camera and easy to work with on it, Kayla shoots swimwear, lingerie and fashion campaigns, and is available for brand appearances, hosting and events.

Studio editorial, a poolside swim line, or a brand activation on the Strip, she shows up camera-ready and brings energy that translates on screen.
